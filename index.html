About Henry

Hello, I'm Henry!

I graduated from Toronto Metropolitan University's Master of Spatial Analysis program.

I have a strong background in planning, spatial analysis, and GIS, with expertise in remote sensing, photogrammetry, and data visualization. Proficient in industry-standard tools such as QGIS, AutoCAD, ArcGIS Pro, MS Office, Adobe Creative Suite, and SketchUp, I excel at transforming complex spatial data into actionable insights. My technical skills include programming in Java, Python, C/C++, SQL (Postgres), JavaScript, HTML/CSS, R, SPSS, and Bash, enabling me to develop custom GIS solutions and automate workflows efficiently. Additionally, I am Advanced RPAS and ROC-A ( Restricted Operator Certificate - Aeronantical) certified, allowing me to integrate drone technology into geospatial analysis and data collection.
